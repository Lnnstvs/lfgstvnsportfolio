Health

PBSP endeavors to improve the overall well-being of Filipinos through interventions that provide poor Filipinos access to basic health services focused on reducing maternal and child mortality, improving nutrition among children, and detecting and curing tuberculosis.


ACCESS TB PROJECT

We seek to reduce TB prevalence, incidence, and mortality by providing comprehensive quality TB care among Filipinos.

SAFE MOTHERHOOD

We educate women of reproductive age on Maternal, Neonatal and Child Health and Nutrition (MNCHN).

SUPPLEMENTAL FEEDING

We provide healthy and nutritious meals to undernourished children in schools and communities.

Education

Every Filipino has a right to accessible and quality education. PBSP works with its members, partners, and donors in improving quality education for learners.


CLASSROOM CONSTRUCTION

We work with donors and partners to provide schools with disaster-resilient, PWD-inclusive and gender-sensitive classrooms.

LEARNING CONTINUITY AND READY FOR SCHOOL
